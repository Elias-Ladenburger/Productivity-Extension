Productivity Booster

Re-take control of your productivity! This extension will redirect you from sites that kill your motivation and productivity and instead redirect you to more productive places.
Apply proven methods taken from “Atomic Habits” by James Clear. Become more focused with the Pomodoro-technique. Finish what you started and feel good about it.

Privacy Policy

This plugin does not collect personal information.

This plugin stores all data visible in the "Productivity Rules" tab and the "Working Times" tab. All data is stored locally on the device of the user.

No data is processed on any device other than the user's browser. Additional data may have been sent to third parties from which this plugin was obtained.
