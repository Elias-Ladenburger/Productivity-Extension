Preamble

This app was built with privacy in mind.

Information processed by this app

No personally identifiable information is being requested, stored or processed by this application. This application processes and stores the following data:

  • Which websites are considered "unproductive" by the user
  • Times which the user considers "work times".
Additionally, this websites processes, but does not store, the following information:
  • Websites visited by the user.

Storage of information

All information is processed and stored on the device of the user.

Information processed during installation or technical support

Depending on the means used to obtaining this app, personally identifiable information may be processed by a third party. This is not endorsed nor in any way facilitated by this app.

Furthermore, personally identifiable information may be transmitted if the user chooses to reach out to the maintainer of this app for the purpose of providing feedback, reporting an error or requesting technical support. This information will be stored for a maximum of two years and deleted thereafter.

What are your data protection rights?

As a natural person and visitor of this website, you have various rights with regards to your privacy:

  • The right to access: You have the right to request I for copies of your personal data. I may charge you a small fee for this service.
  • The right to rectification: You have the right to request that I correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request I to complete the information you believe is incomplete.
  • The right to erasure: You have the right to request that I erase your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to restrict processing: You have the right to request that I restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to object to processing: You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to data portability: You have the right to request that I transfer the data that I have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.

If you make a request, I have to respond to you within one month. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact me via the mail address provided in the imprint.
